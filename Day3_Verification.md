## Reflection

The claim that looked most authoritative but was actually weakest was claim #4: "Anurag University achieved over 2100 placement offers for the Class of 2026." Gemini cited the statistic confidently, and Perplexity initially confirmed it. However, when I checked the primary source, I found that the figure was reported as "2100+ offers and counting" rather than a finalized placement total. This showed that AI tools can present information with high confidence even when details remain uncertain. The lesson is that confidence does not equal correctness, and every important claim should be verified using the original source.
